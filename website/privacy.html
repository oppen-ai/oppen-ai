Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 April 2026

The short version: Öppen AI chat runs entirely in your browser, on your device. Your conversations, prompts, and model inputs never touch our servers — because we don't have any for the chat. This policy covers the small amount of data we do process when you visit this marketing site.

1. Who we are

Öppen AI is an open-source project that provides a truly private, on-device AI chat. This privacy policy applies to oppen.ai (this marketing site) and explains what data we process when you visit it.

For privacy questions or requests, contact: privacy@oppen.ai.

2. What the chat does (and does not) collect

The Öppen AI chat application at chat.oppen.ai runs the AI model directly in your browser using WebGPU / WebAssembly. This means:

  • Your prompts, conversations, and any files you share stay on your device.
  • We do not run inference on servers. We have no backend that receives your chat data.
  • We cannot read, log, or train on your conversations — they never leave your machine.

3. What this marketing site collects

On oppen.ai we use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate visitor traffic (pageviews, country, referrer, device type). This is strictly optional and only runs if you accept analytics cookies via the cookie banner.

When enabled, Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser and sends the following to Google:

  • Pages visited and time on page
  • Anonymized IP address (we enable IP anonymization)
  • Browser, device type, operating system, approximate location (country/region)
  • Referring site

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any directly identifying information on this site. We do not run any advertising or remarketing.

4. Cookies we use

  • cc_cookie (strictly necessary) — stores your cookie-consent choice so we don't ask again. Set by the consent banner itself. Duration: 6 months.
  • _ga, _ga_* (analytics, optional) — set by Google Analytics only if you accept. Used to distinguish unique visitors. Duration: up to 13 months.

5. Legal basis (GDPR)

We rely on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) for analytics cookies. Strictly necessary cookies (just the consent choice itself) are processed under our legitimate interest in remembering your preference.

6. Data retention

Google Analytics is configured with a 14-month retention window for user and event data. After that, aggregate data is deleted automatically. We store no analytics data on our own servers.

7. Third parties

  • Google (Google Analytics 4) — analytics provider. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Amazon Web Services (S3) — static hosting for this site. Standard server access logs may include your IP address transiently.
  • jsDelivr — CDN for the cookie-consent library. Your browser fetches a JS/CSS file from their CDN.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR and similar laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (see below)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@oppen.ai.

9. Withdrawing consent

You can change your cookie choices at any time by clicking Cookie preferences here or in the footer of any page. Rejecting analytics immediately disables Google Analytics and clears its cookies on your next visit.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the project evolves. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes that affect how we process data, we'll surface a notice on the site.
