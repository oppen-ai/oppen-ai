About Öppen AI

Private AI that actually stays private - open in every sense of the word.

Ö

Say hi to Ö. The two dots above are a Swedish umlaut - and, if you look at them for a second, a little pair of friendly eyes peering back at you. Private. Local. Yours.

What "Öppen" means

Öppen (pronounced roughly /ˈœpːɛn/ with rounded lips) is the Swedish word for open. We picked it because the project is open in every sense we could think of:

• Open source - the code is MIT-licensed and lives on GitHub. Read it, fork it, audit it, improve it.
• Open weights - we run permissively-licensed community models directly in your browser.
• Open about your data - we don't collect any of it, because the chat runs entirely on your device. There is no backend for us to spy on you with.

The umlaut isn't just for fun - it's a small reminder that this project has a personality and a point of view.

What we actually do

Öppen AI is a static web page that loads an LLM and runs inference inside your browser, using WebGPU and WebAssembly. Your prompts, your conversations, and any files you share with the model never leave your machine. We do not operate servers that see your chat. We do not train models on your data. We do not have accounts, because we don't need to know who you are.

Who we are

Öppen AI is an independent open-source project maintained by community. There is no company, no investors, no hosted service, and no business model. If that changes, this page will change too.
